## What is Tablr?

Tablr is a complete database management solution that brings the power of AI to your Supabase projects. Using the Model Context Protocol (MCP), Tablr enables you to manage your database through natural language commands in AI assistants like Claude Desktop.

## Getting Started

Ready to transform your database workflow? Here's what you need:

1. **Node.js 18+** and npm installed
2. **Active Supabase project** with service role key
3. **Tablr API key** from the dashboard
4. **MCP-compatible AI assistant** (Claude Desktop, Cursor, etc.)

<Note>
Tablr works seamlessly with any MCP-compatible AI assistant, providing 16 comprehensive database management tools.
</Note>

## Why Choose Tablr?

**Natural Language Database Management** - Instead of writing SQL queries or navigating dashboards, simply ask your AI assistant to manage your database.

**Secure and Reliable** - Built with enterprise-grade security, including SQL injection protection and operation confirmations.

**Developer-Friendly** - Comprehensive error handling, performance monitoring, and TypeScript support.

**MCP Standard** - Works with any MCP-compatible AI assistant, ensuring future compatibility.
